Shop Street

Galway has many unique stores, where you can find a perfect gift that will remind you of the good times you spent here. On Shop Street you can enjoy live music and many other form of local art.

Irish Pubs

Pubs are one of the biggest part of Irish culture. You can enjoy food with your family and taste creamy pint in a cosy and friendly place. Visiting Galway pubs is a must!

Visit Cliffs of Moher

One of the most favorite tourist attractions in Ireland. Every year over one million guests come to enjoy astonishing view. Cliffs of Moher ascend to 214 meters and are 14 km long.
